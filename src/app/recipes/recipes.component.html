Easy Milk Barfi Recipe – Milk Cake – Dessert Recipe

For this milk cake recipe you only need milk and sugar, no other ingredients are required. Depending on different place this milk recipe is also known as milk barfi or simply barfi recipe. To make the milk dessert recipe you have to control heat carefully otherwise milk barfi will burn and you will not get perfect taste from it. For this burfi without milk powder at first use medium flame until you get comparatively thicker milk. After that you have to use low flame until you get final dough.
